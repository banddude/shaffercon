Smart, scalable systems

Permits and Title 24 expertise
Shaffer Construction handles all LADBS permitting, Title 24 energy compliance documentation, and inspection coordination. We prepare electrical plans, lighting schedules, controls narratives, single-line diagrams, and NRCC forms, and we coordinate certified lighting controls acceptance testing (LCAT) through qualified technicians. Our installations meet California Electrical Code (CEC), Title 24 Part 6 (energy), CALGreen, and any applicable local amendments. By working proactively with LADBS plan review and inspectors, we prevent delays, secure approvals faster, and ensure your project passes on the first inspection—saving time and money.
Designs tailored to your business
Every Pacific Palisades commercial property is different. We start with a comprehensive energy audit and walk-through, documenting load profiles, existing lighting and controls, panel capacity, and operational schedules. We analyze utility interval data (SCE or LADWP) to understand demand patterns and time-of-use exposure. Photometric modeling ensures code-compliant, visually comfortable lighting for offices, retail color rendering for boutiques and restaurants, and safe illumination for parking areas. You'll receive options with budget tiers, phasing plans, ROI projections including utility incentives, and minimal-disruption installation schedules.
